# Welcome to SWIFTSELL

SWIFTSELL is a web-based **Configure, Price, Quote (CPQ)** platform that helps your sales team build accurate quotes faster, manage customer relationships, and process orders — all in one place.

## What can you do with SWIFTSELL?

- **Browse the product catalog** — search and filter available products, view pricing and specifications.
- **Configure products** — use the guided configurator to select options and build a product to your customer's exact needs.
- **Build and manage quotes** — add configured products as line items, apply pricing, attach supporting documents, and communicate with your team.
- **Submit orders** — check out directly from a quote and track the order through fulfillment.
- **Manage returns** — submit and track Return Merchandise Authorizations (RMAs).
