# Creating a Quote

There are two ways to start a new quote in SWIFTSELL.

## Option 1: Start from the Quote Dashboard

1. Navigate to **Quotes** in the main navigation.
2. Click **New Quote**.
3. SWIFTSELL creates a blank quote and opens it immediately.
4. From the **Lines** tab, configure and add products.

## Option 2: Start from the Product Catalog

1. Navigate to **Product Catalog**.
2. Find the product you want and click **Configure**.
3. SWIFTSELL creates a new quote automatically and opens the [Configurator](../configurator).
4. Complete your configuration — when you click **Finish**, the product is added as Line 1 of your new quote.

## Option 3: Quick Configure (if available)

If your administrator has enabled Quick Configure products, they appear as a product grid at the top of the Quote Dashboard. Click any product card to immediately open the Configurator with a new quote.

## After Creating a Quote

Once a quote exists, you can:

- Add more lines by using the **Configure** action from the Lines tab.
- Fill in customer and address details on the **Customer** tab.
- Attach supporting files on the **Lines** tab.
- Print or check out from the **Summary** tab.
