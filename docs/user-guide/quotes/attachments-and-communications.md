# Attachments

The **Attachments** section appears at the bottom of the **Lines** tab on a quote. Use it to upload supporting documents such as drawings, specifications, or customer-provided files.

## Uploading an Attachment

1. Open the quote and go to the **Lines** tab.
2. Scroll to the **Attachments** section at the bottom of the page.
3. Click **Upload** (or drag and drop a file into the upload area).
4. The file is attached to the quote immediately.

## Viewing Attachments

Uploaded files are listed with their filename, size, and upload date. Click a file name to download or preview it.

## Removing an Attachment

Click the **delete icon** next to an attachment to remove it. Attachments on orders (converted quotes) are read-only and cannot be removed.

:::note
Attachments on a quote (type = Quote) can be managed freely. Once the quote becomes an **Order**, attachments are read-only.
:::
