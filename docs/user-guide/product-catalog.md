# Product Catalog

The Product Catalog is the starting point for building a quote. It lists all products available on your platform and can be browsed without logging in.

## Browsing the Catalog

Navigate to the **Product Catalog** from the main navigation bar. Products are displayed in a grid or list view.

## Searching and Filtering

- Use the **search bar** to find products by name or keyword.
- Use the **filter panel** to narrow results by product group or category.
- Toggle between **grid view** and **list view** using the view controls.

## Product Cards

Each product card shows:

- Product name and image
- Base price
- A short description

Click a product card to open the **Product Preview**, which shows full details before you start configuring.

## Starting a Configuration

From the catalog or product preview, click **Configure** to open the product in the [Configurator](./configurator). This will either add the product to an existing quote or prompt you to create a new one.

:::tip Quick Configure

If your administrator has set up Quick Configure products, they will appear at the top of your [Quote Dashboard](./quotes/overview) for fast one-click access.

:::
