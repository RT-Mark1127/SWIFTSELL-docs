# Logging In

## Accessing SWIFTSELL

Open SWIFTSELL in your web browser and navigate to your organization's SWIFTSELL URL.

## Standard Login

1. On the login screen, enter your **username** and **password**.
2. Click **Log In**.
3. If your account requires two-factor authentication (2FA), you will be prompted to complete that step before continuing.

## Forgot Your Password?

1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login screen.
2. Enter your email address and click **Send Reset Link**.
3. Check your email for a password reset link and follow the instructions.

:::note
Password reset emails are sent from your organization's configured SMTP address. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder or contact your administrator.
:::

## First-Time Registration

If your organization allows self-registration:

1. Click **Register** on the login screen.
2. Fill in your name, email, and a password.
3. If 2FA enrollment is required, you will be guided through that setup immediately after registering.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

SWIFTSELL supports 2FA for enhanced account security. If 2FA is enabled for your account:

- You will be prompted to enter a verification code after your password.
- Codes are typically generated by an authenticator app (such as Google Authenticator or Authy).
- If you need to set up or reset 2FA, contact your administrator.

## Logging Out

Click your profile menu in the top navigation bar and select **Log Out**. You can also navigate directly to `/auth/logout`.
