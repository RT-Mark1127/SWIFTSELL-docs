# Product Groups

**Product Groups** organize products into categories for filtering and display purposes in the catalog.

:::note
Product Groups must be enabled via the **Feature Flags** section before they appear in Administration.
:::

## Accessing Product Groups

Go to **Administration → Product Groups**. *(Requires the `productGroups` feature flag to be enabled.)*

## Creating a Product Group

1. Click **Create Product Group**.
2. Enter a name for the group.
3. Assign products to the group.
4. Click **Save**.

## Editing a Product Group

Click any group row to open and edit it. You can rename the group or change its product assignments.
